About Us

RPL, or Software Engineering (SE) is a professional field that explores ways of developing software / software including creation, software development / software and quality management. The software engineering (SE) Expertise Program is one of the expertise competencies in the field of Computer and Information Technology which specifically studies computer programming. Today's RPL graduates are needed to answer the many needs of the industry for the availability of technicians in the field of software development. Some of the scope of work in the SE field is Software Developer, Game Development, Software Tester, Software Engineering, Software Analyst and Integrator, IT Consultant and Programmer.
